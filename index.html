About Me

I am currently a RTG postdoctoral fellow in the mathematics department at Northwestern University. Previously I obtained my PhD from the University of Victoria under the supervision of Chris Bose and Anthony Quas. My thesis was awarded a Govenor General's gold medal. Before that I received a B.S. in chemistry and mathematics and a M.S. in pure mathematics from DePaul University.
